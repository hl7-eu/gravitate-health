Fluenz Tetra will help to protect against the four virus strains contained in the vaccine, and other strains closely related to them.</p><p><strong>How Fluenz Tetra works</strong></p><p>When a person is given the vaccine, the immune system (the body’s natural defence system) will produce its own protection against the influenza virus.
